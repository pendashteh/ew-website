## What the Network Is

The Electron Network is not a service desk or a directory. It is a membership collective — a group of organisations and individuals who have opted in to a shared ecosystem, with Electron Workshop holding the connective tissue together.

Members range from small community groups and non-profits to open tech organisations, independent practitioners, hosting providers, and ecosystem enablers like funders and policy advocates. What they share is a commitment to open technology and community benefit — and a willingness to act on it together.

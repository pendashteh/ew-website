<h1 class="mb-4">Community Network Benefits</h1>

<p class="lead text-secondary mb-4">Being part of the Electron Network isn't just about tools — it's about belonging to a federation of like-minded groups who support each other.</p>

**What network membership means:**
- Access to a growing community of community-first organisations
- Shared knowledge: co-learning sessions, peer support, and open discussions
- Optional cross-promotion — let other network groups know what you're working on
- Visibility to the network's providers, sponsors, and collaborators
- Early access to new offerings as they're facilitated into the network

<p class="text-secondary">The network is facilitated by Electron Workshop but shaped by the groups in it. As the network grows, so do the opportunities for collaboration and mutual benefit.</p>

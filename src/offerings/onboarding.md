<h1 class="mb-4">Onboarding &amp; Support</h1>

<p class="lead text-secondary mb-4">Getting started is the hardest part. We help you migrate, configure, and get comfortable — so you're not left to figure it out alone.</p>

**What your group gets:**
- Migration of existing mailing lists and files
- Walkthrough sessions for your coordinators
- First campaign or team space set up together
- Ongoing light-touch support through the network

<p class="text-secondary">Onboarding is coordinated by Electron Workshop in partnership with network service providers. Depending on which tools you're using, your service provider will be involved in the onboarding process.</p>
